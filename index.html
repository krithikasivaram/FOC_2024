Course contents

  • Module 1:
    Fuels and their properties, Review of basic thermodynamics and gaseous mixtures, Combustion Thermodynamics; Stoichiometry, I and II Laws of Thermodynamics applied to combustion - equilibrium composition

  • Module 2:
    Fundamentals of combustion kinetics, Transport properties, Mass transfer - Stefan flow, droplet vaporization, Governing equations for a reacting flow

  • Module 3:
    Non-premixed flames, premixed flames, limit phenomena - flammability limits, quenching of laminar flames, ignition, flame stabilization

  • Module 4:
    Combustion of gaseous fuel jets, Turbulent premixed and non-premixed flames, Combustion of a carbon particle
